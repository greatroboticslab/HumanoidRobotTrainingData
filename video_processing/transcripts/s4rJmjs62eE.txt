 Keeping chickens cool. It's been getting hot in Ohio so I wanted to build a structure that could help keep the birds cool in these hot summer days. All it took was three cattle panels, a 12 by 20 shade cloth, some zip ties, and four cedar 2x4s. Now the birds are absolutely loving it.
