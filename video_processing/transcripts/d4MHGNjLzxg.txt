 People don't realize part of our job here on the farm is to ensure safety of everyone. My family grows before the season from a family of five to over 80 and my job is to ensure that everyone is happy, healthy and safe here on the farm. And my responsibility as a mom to my farm family includes things like making sure everyone has rain boots to booking doctor's appointments and dental appointments.
