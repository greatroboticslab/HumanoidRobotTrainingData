 This is a vertical aeroponic tower garden. It has a capacity of growing up to 44 plants within less than one square meter, which is approximately 10 square feet. And you can grow up to 160 different types of crops. You can mix and match them on the tower. And the great thing about the tower garden is that you use 95% less water than traditional farming methods.
