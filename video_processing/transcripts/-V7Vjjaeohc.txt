 Ever wondered how the green revolution transformed farming practices? It all started with the introduction of high-yielding crop varieties. These crops produced significantly higher yields than traditional ones. The use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides further boosted crop growth. This combination revolutionized agriculture, increasing food production globally.
