 Did you know that the Obano civilization in the Amazon flourished due to their advanced agricultural techniques? They created raised fields and water reservoirs to improve crop production in the rich Amazonian soil. They also built a complex water system consisting of canals and storage ponds, which helped in irrigation and fishing. Their remains, discovered in areas like Bolivia and Brazil, include large architectural structures used in ceremonial rituals, featuring platforms and large stone circles.
