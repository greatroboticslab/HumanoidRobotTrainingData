 Many incorporate bread making into their rituals, seeing it as a symbolic way to honor the earth's bounty. Crafting corn dollies, a traditional folk art representing the spirit of the grain, is another common practice. Our journey through the wheel of the year brings us to Mabon, the autumn equinox celebration. Named after a figure from Welsh mythology, Mabon is a modern pagan name for this festival of balance and thanksgiving.
