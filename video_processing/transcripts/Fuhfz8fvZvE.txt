Education
 Agricultural practices gives you a chance to explore, experience and learn knowledge and practical skills valued in farming. You'll learn about the management practices of animal and plant industries in hands-on ways. You should study agricultural practices if you're interested in working in agriculture including careers in agribusiness, aquaculture, food technology or environmental management.
